  1. We can write the answer as \(P(23,16) = 23 \cdot 22 \cdot 21 \cdot \dots \cdot 8\) , which is the same as \(\frac{23!}{7!}\) . Or, if you think of picking the 16 books and then arranging those 16, you can write this as \(\binom{23}{16}\cdot 16!\) . Note, that since any order is acceptable, we are distinguishing between different orders, so a permutation is appropriate here.
