Consider the function \(f:\{1,2,3,4,5\} \to \{1,2,3,4,5\}\) given by
\(\displaystyle{f = \begin{pmatrix} 1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 4 \amp 5 \\ 1 \amp 5 \amp 3 \amp 2 \amp 3 \end{pmatrix}.}\)
  1. Find \(f(2)\text{.}\)
  2. Find a \(n\) in the domain such that \(f(n) = 2\text{.}\)
  3. Find an element \(n\) of the domain such that \(f(n) = n\text{.}\)
  4. Find an element of the codomain that is not in the range.
