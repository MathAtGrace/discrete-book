Solution 1.5.6.1.

\({10 \choose 5}\) outcomes are possible in traditional Yahtzee. Each die is a star, its value is determined by where it is put relative to the bars.
For Super-Yahtzee, \({10 \choose 3}\) outcomes. We have 7 stars (the 7 dice) and 3 bars (the 3 switches between the numbers 1-4).
