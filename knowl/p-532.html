\(A \cap B\) will be the set of natural numbers that are both at least 3 and less than 16, and even. That is, \(\renewcommand{\d}{\displaystyle}\newcommand{\N}{\mathbb N}\newcommand{\st}{:}\renewcommand{\v}{\vtx{above}{}}A \cap B = \{x \in \N \st 3 \le x \lt 16 \wedge x \text{ is even}\} = {\left\{4,6,8,10,12,14\right\}}\text{.}\)
