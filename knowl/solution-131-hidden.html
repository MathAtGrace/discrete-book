We must figure out how many different combinations of 5 coins are possible. Let a star represent each coin, and a bar represent switching between type of coin. For example, if we have 7 coins, \(**|*||****\) represents 2 pennies, one nickel, no dimes and 4 quarters. The number of such star and bar diagrams for 8 total stars and bars (with 5 stars and 3 bars) is \({8 \choose 5} = {0.0178571}\text{.}\) Thus you have a 1 in 0.0178571 chance of guessing correctly.
