How many functions \(f: {\left\{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9\right\}} \to {\left\{1,2,3,4,5,6,7,8,9\right\}}\) are there satisfying:
  1. \(f(1) = 1\) or \(f(2) = 2\) (or both)?
  2. \(f(1) \ne 1\) or \(f(2) \ne 2\) (or both)?
  3. \(f(1) \ne 1\) and \(f(2) \ne 2\text{,}\) and \(f\) is injective?
  4. \(f\) is surjective, but \(\forall x \in \{1,2,\dots, 9 \} \text{, } f(x) \ne x\text{?}\)
