Exercise 4.

Determine whether each molecular statement below is true or false, or whether it is impossible to determine. Assume you do not know what my favorite number is (but you do know which numbers are prime).
  1. 7 is prime and 16 is not prime
  2. If 7 is not prime, then 7 is my favorite number.
  3. If 16 is my favorite number, then \(16+1\) is my favorite number
  4. 16 is prime or 7 is prime
  5. 17 is my favorite number and 16 is not prime.
  6. If 16 is not prime, then 16 is my favorite number.
