  2. If you will give me a cow, then I will not give you magic beans.
  3. If I will give you magic beans, then you will not give me a cow.
  4. If I will give you magic beans, then you will give me a cow.
  5. You will give me a cow and I will not give you magic beans.
  6. If I will not give you magic beans, then you will not give me a cow.
