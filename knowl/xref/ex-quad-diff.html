Exercise 5.

Suppose Suppose \(a_n = {2n^{2}-4n+2}\text{.}\) Find a closed formula for the sequence of differences by computing \(a_n - a_{n-1}\text{.}\) Simplify your answer as much as posible.
\(a_n - a_{n-1} =\)
Solution.
\(a_{n-1} = (n-1)^2 - 4(n-1) + 2 = {2n^{2}-8n+8}\text{.}\) Thus \(a_n - a_{n-1} = {4n-6}\text{.}\) Note that this is linear (arithmetic).
