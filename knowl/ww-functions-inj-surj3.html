Exercise 4.

Consider the following functions \(f: \lbrace 1,2,3,4\rbrace \to \lbrace 1,2,3,4,5\rbrace\text{.}\) For each, determine whether it is (only) injective, (only) surjective, bijective, or neither injective nor surjective.
  1. \(\displaystyle f = \begin{pmatrix}1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 4 \\ 1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 4\end{pmatrix}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle f = \begin{pmatrix}1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 4 \\ 5 \amp 4 \amp 3 \amp 2\end{pmatrix}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle f = \begin{pmatrix}1 \amp 2 \amp 3 \amp 4 \\ 5 \amp 1 \amp 3 \amp 1\end{pmatrix}\)
